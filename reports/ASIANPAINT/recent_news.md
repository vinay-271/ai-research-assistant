### May 30, 2026
*   **Source:** *The Economic Times*
*   **Summary:** **Asian Paints Q4 Net Profit Surges 69% to ₹1,172 Crore on Volume Recovery**
    Asian Paints reported a consolidated net profit of ₹1,172.12 Crore for the fourth quarter ended March 31, 2026, marking a 69.3% increase compared to Q4 FY25.
