## Revenue
For the fourth quarter (Q4) ended March 31, 2026, HDFC Bank reported a standalone net revenue of ₹46,280 Crore (₹462.8 billion), showing a 4.9% growth compared to ₹44,100 Crore (₹441.0 billion) in Q4 FY25.
*   **Net Interest Income (NII):** NII grew 3.2% year-on-year (and 1.4% sequentially) to ₹33,080 Crore (₹330.8 billion), reflecting stable credit yields.
*   **Non-Interest Income:** Stood at ₹13,200 Crore (₹132.0 billion), marking a 9.7% increase year-on-year, driven by stable core retail fee streams.

## PAT
HDFC Bank reported a standalone Profit After Tax (PAT) of ₹19,221 Crore (₹192.2 billion) for Q4 FY26, registering an increase of 9.1% year-on-year compared to ₹17,620 Crore (₹176.2 billion) in Q4 FY25. On a sequential basis, standalone PAT grew by 3.1% from Q3 FY26's net profit of ₹18,650 Crore (₹186.5 billion).

## EPS
For the quarter ended March 31, 2026, standalone basic Earnings Per Share (EPS) was reported at ₹12.50 per share, up 8.7% compared to ₹11.50 in the corresponding period of the previous fiscal year.

## Key operational highlights
*   **Steady Liability Accumulation:** Deposits expanded sequentially by 8.6% (and 14.4% YoY) to end the quarter at ₹31.05 Lakh Crore (₹31,053 billion). Average deposits grew 12.8% year-on-year.
*   **Credit Book Growth:** Standalone advances under management stood at ₹29.37 Lakh Crore (₹29,372 billion), up 10.0% year-on-year and 3.5% sequentially.
*   **NIM Profile:** Standalone Net Interest Margin (NIM) was stable quarter-on-quarter, standing at 3.38% on total assets.
*   **Operating Leverage:** Total operating expenses fell 1.6% sequentially to ₹18,480 Crore (₹184.8 billion), helping maintain the core cost-to-income ratio at 39.9%.
*   **Asset Quality and Credit Cost:** Standalone Gross NPA ratio stood flat at 1.20%, and ex-agri GNPA was down to 0.91%. Quarterly credit provisioning fell 18.2% year-on-year to ₹2,610 Crore (₹26.1 billion).
*   **Dividend Distribution:** The Board recommended a final dividend of ₹13 per equity share for the full fiscal year ended March 31, 2026.

## Management commentary
Sashidhar Jagdishan, Managing Director and CEO, commented on the earnings:

"Our performance during the fourth quarter of FY26 indicates that we are moving steadily through our post-merger integration phase. Standalone net profit grew by 9% to ₹19,221 crore, supported by disciplined cost management and a reduction in our credit provisions. 

Our focus over the past year has been liability gathering, which is reflected in our 8.6% sequential deposit expansion during this quarter. While margin environment across the banking sector remains competitive, our Core Cost-to-Income was managed at 39.9%, demonstrating our operational efficiency. Our credit metrics remain robust with GNPA at 1.20%. We are focused on rebalancing our loan-to-deposit ratios, streamlining our cost of funds, and continuing to build long-term relationships with our retail and corporate clients."

## Future guidance
*   **LDR and Deposit Gathering:** The bank expects credit and deposits to compound at 12% and 13% annually over the FY26-28 period. To support this, management aims to mobilize between ₹4.0 and ₹4.5 lakh crore in fresh deposits over the next two years.
*   **Borrowing Reductions:** HDFC Bank plans to utilize its strong deposit inflows to systematically retire high-cost wholesale borrowings accumulated during the parent merger, helping to stabilize Net Interest Margins.
*   **RoA and RoE Targets:** Over the medium term, the bank aims to keep its Return on Assets (RoA) close to its historical benchmark of 1.9%–2.1% and Return on Equity (RoE) in the range of 14.5%–16.0%.
