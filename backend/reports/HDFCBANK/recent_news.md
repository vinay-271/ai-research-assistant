### March 18, 2026
*   **Source:** *The Hindu*
*   **Summary:** **HDFC Bank Chairman Atanu Chakraborty Resigns over Ethical and Value Differences**
    Atanu Chakraborty, the Part-time Chairman and Independent Director of HDFC Bank, tendered his resignation with immediate effect. In his resignation letter, Chakraborty stated that "certain happenings and practices within the bank over the past two years were not in congruence with my personal values and ethics". He noted that while the HDFC Ltd merger was a major milestone, "the benefits are yet to fully fructify". The bank confirmed his resignation to stock exchanges, stating there were no other material reasons.
